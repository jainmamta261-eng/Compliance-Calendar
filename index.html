How to Use the Compliance Calendar

FY 2026–27 · Step-by-step guide

Navigating the calendar
1
Open the file. Open compliance_calendar.html in any web browser.
2
Select a quarter. The home page shows four quarter cards — Q1 (Apr–Jun), Q2 (Jul–Sep), Q3 (Oct–Dec) and Q4 (Jan–Mar). Click any card to open that quarter's calendar.
3
Read the calendar. Compliance dates are underlined and marked with coloured dots. Each colour represents a different law.
4
View details. Click any underlined date to see a full list of compliance items due on that day.
5
Jump between quarters. Quick-access buttons for the other three quarters appear at the top of each quarter view.
6
Search. Click the Search button on the home page to find any compliance item instantly by form name or description.
7
Download PDFs. Use the coloured download buttons on the home page to download law-specific compliance schedules.
8
Act Wise view. Click Act Wise on the home page to browse compliance by law. Use Q1-Q4 tabs to switch quarters. Click any month name to see that month's deadlines for that act.
⚠️ Disclaimer: This calendar is for informational purposes only. Due dates are subject to change. Always verify on official portals — incometax.gov.in, gst.gov.in, mca.gov.in, rbi.org.in — or consult your CA before filing.
